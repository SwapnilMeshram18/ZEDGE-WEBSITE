SERVICES

Every journey is different, and so are the challenges that come with it. Our services are designed to support you through moments of uncertainty, growth, and change, offering thoughtful guidance tailored to your unique needs

Career Counselling
1
First Meeting

We start with a real conversation

Before any assessment or advice, we listen. In your first session, we create a calm, unhurried space for you to share where you are right now — what's working, what isn't, and what's been weighing on you. There's no agenda to rush through. We want to understand you as a whole person, not just your CV.
2
Self-Discovery

Mapping your strengths, values & interests

Using structured reflection exercises and, where useful, validated assessment tools, we help you get a clearer picture of who you are professionally. We explore what genuinely energises you, what you're naturally good at, and what kind of work environment lets you thrive, not just survive. Many clients are surprised by what this reveals.
3
Exploration

Exploring what's actually possible for you

This is where we expand the horizon. Together, we look at career options, industries, and roles that align with your strengths and values — including paths you may not have considered yet. We help you separate fear-driven thinking from genuine misalignment, and challenge assumptions that may be keeping you stuck. No option is too big or too unconventional to explore.
4
Decision Making

Making sense of your options, without the overwhelm

Having options is only helpful if you can actually decide. We work through the practical and emotional sides of your choices — weighing trade-offs, addressing fears, and cutting through the noise. You won't be handed a prescription. Instead, you'll develop your own clarity, so the decision you make is one you genuinely own and believe in.
5
Defining Next Steps

A clear, honest plan for what comes next

Clarity without action stays a feeling. We help you build a concrete, realistic plan — broken into steps that feel manageable, not overwhelming. Whether that's updating your skills, having a difficult conversation, or making a bold leap, we'll define the next three moves together. You leave with direction, not just insight.

A space where you can feel truly seen and make sense of what you're going through.
