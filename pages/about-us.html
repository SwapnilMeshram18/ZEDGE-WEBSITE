ABOUT US

OUR FOUNDER

Meet Renu Daga,
Founder of Z-Edge Counselling

With over 30+ years of dedicated professional experience, Renu Daga has helped hundreds of individuals and families find clear directions in their personal lives, relationships, and professional careers.

At Z-Edge Counselling in Nagpur, our philosophy is simple: we do not define you; we guide you to discover your own unique potential. We believe in providing a compassionate, non-judgmental, and highly confidential environment where you can speak your truth and explore paths to growth.
