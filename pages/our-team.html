Meet the Founder

Renu Daga, Founder &

Lead Counsellor, Z-Edge • 30 years of practice

Life moves in every direction, but what traps us is always the depth we haven't yet faced. With three decades of lived counselling experience, Renu Daga has guided individuals across every chapter of life, from the anxiety of exam halls to the quiet grief of loss. Her practice is built on one belief: that every person, when met without barrier or judgment, can find their way back to themselves.

Renu has sat with human beings at their most complicated for thirty years. Not above them, with them. She has heard things said for the very first time. She has watched people discover, in the middle of an ordinary sentence, something true about themselves they had spent years burying. That is the work she has never tired of.

Z-Edge was not born from a curriculum. It was born from the pattern Renu kept seeing: that people don't struggle because they are weak, they struggle because they are deep. And depth, without direction, becomes a trap. Her practice exists to transform that depth into discovery.
